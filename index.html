Policies & Guidelines

Check-In & Check-Out

  • Check-in: 4:00 PM or later (self check-in)
  • Check-out: 11:00 AM
  • Early check-in available upon request ($75 fee)
  • Late check-out by arrangement only

House Rules

  • No smoking inside or on the property
  • No parties or events over 10 guests
  • Pet-friendly (2 dogs max, $150 pet fee)
  • Quiet hours 10:00 PM - 8:00 AM
  • All guests must sign rental agreement

Cancellation Policy

  • Full refund if cancelled 60+ days out
  • 50% refund 30-60 days before arrival
  • No refund within 30 days of arrival
  • Travel insurance strongly recommended
  • Force majeure handled case-by-case
